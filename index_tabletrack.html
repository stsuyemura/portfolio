problem statement & hypothesis

Restaurant goers need a way to efficiently track the wait time status of their table because they want to keep informed and less stressed.

We believe that by creating a way to track the status of your table for restaurant goers we will achieve more peace of mind for patrons therefore increasing the likelihood of them having a positive experience.

We know this to be true when there is an a 10% increase in repeat business and positive reviews.

research

target audience

Individuals who like to go out to eat but are often bothered when they have to wait after putting their name down.
