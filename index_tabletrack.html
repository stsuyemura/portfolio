ABOUT WORK CONTACT

Restaurants' Waitlists Made Easy

Making last minute plans to go out to eat can often be stressful. TableTrack simplifies the process for patrons, making it easy to add and track your name on a restaurant waitlist.

Role: UX Designer

Problem Statement & Hypothesis

Restaurant goers need a way to efficiently track the wait time status of their table because they want to keep informed and less stressed.

We believe that by creating a way to track the status of your table for restaurant goers we will achieve more peace of mind for patrons therefore increasing the likelihood of them having a positive experience.

We know this to be true when there is an a 10% increase in repeat business and positive reviews.

Research

Target Audience

Individuals who like to go out to eat but are often bothered when they have to wait after putting their name down.

User Interviews

How interviews were conducted

  • 7 users, ranging from 22-60 years old
  • All shared a love of food and going out to eat
  • Asked about past restaurant waiting experiences

Goals of Interviews

  • Find emotions behind the wait experience
  • Likes & dislikes that are associated with the wait experience

User Goals

  • Keep informed of wait status
  • Finding a restaurant that fits needs (ratings and proximity)
  • Open communication with restaurant
