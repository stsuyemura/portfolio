Messaging Customers Day-of

Sometimes uncontrollable circumstances can throw a wrench in daily operations of a small business. The ability to easily send a last minute message to their customers can free up time for day-to-day tasks.

Role: UX Designer

Problem Statement & Hypothesis

Small businesses need a way to easily message their incoming customers because they want to more time to work on their day-to-day operations.

We believe that by creating a way to message selected customers for small businesses they will achieve more effective business practices and minimize the amount of time spent on manual notification.

Research

Target Audience

Small businesses who are faced with last minute changes that could affect their operations.

User Goals

  • Messaging groups of customers for specific days
  • Composing and saving messages easily
  • Adjusting the recipients of the message
