About me

Stephanie is a designer born and based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Her passion for all things visual started at the age of 9 when she began oil painting classes. Don’t ask her to see them as some are quite embarrassing. :)

She is a true believer in design changing the way we look at the world, and prides herself in using it as a way to tell a story. When she isn’t working, she loves to bake and DIY.
